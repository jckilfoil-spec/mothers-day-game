Privacy

This is a personal Mother’s Day game made for friends & family. No commercial use, no data resale.

What’s collected

Anonymous analytics: page views, button clicks, level progress, time on each screen, and session recordings (with all text inputs masked). Powered by PostHog.

What’s not collected

No real names, no emails, no addresses, no location, no photos, and nothing else that identifies you personally. The photos and names you give characters live only in your browser’s localStorage — they never leave your device. If you choose to type your name into the optional feedback form, that submission is sent separately (via Tally), not joined with analytics.

How to opt out

Tap “Decline” on the cookie banner, clear this site’s localStorage, or use any browser ad-blocker. No analytics will be captured.
